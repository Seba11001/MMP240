Upstate New York

If you're not from the state of New York, when you think of it, all that comes to mind is the city. However there is so much more to this beautiful state. Hardly anyone cares to mention that there are beautiful landscapes just north of the city just waiting to be explored and photographed. It's a great weekend getaway for anyone that is in the city wanting to get a break from the concrete jungle that is New York City. These hikes featured on this page are all within driving distance from New York City, and when you get there you could hardly believe that you're in the same state as the Big Apple!
