About:

I am an 18 year-old hiker and aspiring computer engineer trying to find the best of both worlds. I often like to combine my passions of the outdoors and computers by making projects such as this. Anytime I could expose the amazing world of the wilderness makes me happy as I have the posiblilty of making a new hiker. We could all use a little bit of the outdoors in our lives andI'm trying to play a part in helping people discover that. Even if it's one person at a time.
